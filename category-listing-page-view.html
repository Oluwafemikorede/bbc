Thrilled Queen visits baby prince

27 July, 2013 14:30
Good Morning Oloba, Below is her (Intel Teach) profile and also attached is her photograph: Assistant Director of Education, Fed. Min. of Education; A Senior Trainer of Intel Education Program; An Innovative Educator of Microsoft Partners In Learning. I am a change agent of the Education Sector particularly an advocate of the 21st Century Teaching and Learning Approaches.

I have trained many teachers in Federal Government Colleges and Colleges of Education in developing 21st century skills and creating a 21st century classroom environment. In addition is the use of ICT - (web 2.0) as a tool for teaching and the Project Based Learning (PBL) methods of the 21st century educator.

I have coordinated many international collaboration with students of Queens College Yaba, Lagos and Fed Govt. College Lagos with their peers abroad.
